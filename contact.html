Frequently Asked Questions

How can I read free books?

You can browse our collection of free books on the "Services" page under the "Free Reading" category. Simply click "Read Now" on any free book to start reading immediately in your browser. No account is required for free books!

How do I purchase a book?

To purchase a book, you need to create an account and log in. Find the book you want, add it to your cart from the "Services" page, and proceed to checkout. We accept various payment methods including UPI, credit/debit cards, and Cash on Delivery.

Can I read purchased books on multiple devices?

Yes! Once you purchase a book, it is added to your account's library. You can access and read your purchased books from any device by logging into your BookHome account.

What is your return policy?

Due to the digital nature of our products, we do not offer returns or refunds on purchased e-books. However, if you experience a technical issue with your purchase, please contact our support team, and we will be happy to assist you.
